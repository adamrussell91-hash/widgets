Why rate confidence? Rating before you write builds metacognitive calibration — the ability to know what you know. After you mark your own answer, you'll set a "how well did you do" rating and see the gap between the two. That gap is the most precise signal about where to focus practice next. Without it, you can spend hours practising the wrong things.

Structure guide
  1. State your main idea and link it to the concept in the question.
  2. Name a specific technique and quote briefly from the text.
  3. Explain the precise effect of that technique on the reader.
  4. Link back to the question's concept in your own words.
