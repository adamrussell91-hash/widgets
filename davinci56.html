Word Origins
Where Do These Words Come From?
👆 Click any word box
to learn more about it

Both machine and mechanics trace back to the same ancient root: the Proto-Indo-European word *mēkh-. Thousands of years ago, this word meant "a clever way around a problem" — and that idea is still baked into both words today!
