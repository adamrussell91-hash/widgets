⚔ Curse Bingo ⚔

Richard III — Margaret's Prophecies

How to play: As the class reads through Richard III, tick off each square when Margaret's curse is fulfilled in that scene. The teacher pauses at curse moments and the class identifies them together. First to complete a full line wins a small prize. Complete the full card and you earn the right to deliver any curse from memory to the room.
🎭 Line complete — the prophecy advances!
👑 Full card! Margaret's vengeance is complete. Now recite a curse from memory! 👑
